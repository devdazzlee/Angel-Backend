1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

BrandBoost Digital is a full-service digital marketing agency specializing in helping small and medium-sized businesses establish and grow their online presence. We provide comprehensive digital marketing services including SEO, social media management, content creation, paid advertising, and web design.

Key Highlights:

  • Target Market: SMBs in the Chicago metropolitan area
  • Initial Investment: $120,000
  • Projected Year 1 Revenue: $350,000
  • Break-even: Month 8
  • Team Size: 5 employees in Year 1

2. COMPANY DESCRIPTION

Company Information

  • Business Name: BrandBoost Digital
  • Legal Structure: Limited Liability Company (LLC)
  • Location: Chicago, Illinois
  • Founded: 2024
  • Industry: Digital Marketing & Advertising Services

Mission Statement

To empower small and medium-sized businesses with professional digital marketing services that drive growth, increase brand awareness, and deliver measurable results.

Vision Statement

To become the leading digital marketing partner for SMBs in the Chicago area, known for our results-driven approach, transparent communication, and commitment to client success.

3. PROBLEM & SOLUTION

Problem Statement

Small and medium-sized businesses face significant challenges in the digital marketplace:

  • Limited marketing budgets compared to large corporations
  • Lack of in-house marketing expertise
  • Difficulty understanding and implementing digital marketing strategies
  • Inability to compete effectively online
  • Wasting money on ineffective marketing efforts

Solution

BrandBoost Digital provides comprehensive, affordable digital marketing services tailored specifically for SMBs:

  • Customized marketing strategies based on business goals
  • Affordable monthly packages ($500-$3,000/month)
  • Transparent reporting and measurable results
  • Expert team with proven track record
  • All-in-one service (no need to hire multiple agencies)

4. MARKET ANALYSIS

Target Market

Primary Target Customers:

  • Small businesses (5-50 employees) in Chicago area
  • Medium-sized businesses (50-200 employees) seeking marketing support
  • Service-based businesses (law firms, accounting firms, consultants)
  • Retail businesses needing online presence
  • E-commerce businesses requiring digital marketing

Target Market Size

  • Total Addressable Market: 85,000 small and medium businesses in Chicago metropolitan area
  • Serviceable Addressable Market: 25,000 businesses actively seeking digital marketing services
  • Serviceable Obtainable Market: 2,500 clients (10% market share goal over 3 years)
  • Year 1 Target: 50-75 clients

Competitive Analysis

Main Competitors:

  • Large Marketing Agencies - Expensive, not SMB-focused
  • Freelance Marketers - Limited expertise, unreliable
  • In-House Marketing Teams - Expensive to maintain
  • DIY Marketing Tools - Time-consuming, limited results

10. RISK ANALYSIS

Major Risks & Challenges

  • Client Acquisition Challenges: Mitigation - Diversified marketing channels, referral program
  • High Client Churn: Mitigation - Focus on results, excellent service, retention programs
  • Economic Downturn: Mitigation - Flexible pricing, value-focused packages
  • Competition from Large Agencies: Mitigation - SMB specialization, competitive pricing
  • Technology Changes: Mitigation - Continuous learning, industry certifications

Contingency Plans

  • 6-month cash reserve for emergencies
  • Diversified client base (no single client >20% revenue)
  • Backup service providers
  • Insurance coverage for major risks
  • Multiple revenue streams
