PROBLEM STATEMENT
-----------------
Current fitness market problems:
- Overcrowded gyms with outdated equipment
- Expensive personal training ($80-150/hour)
- Lack of community and motivation
- Limited class schedules that don't fit busy professionals
- Intimidating gym environments

SOLUTION
--------
FitZone Gym provides:
- Modern, spacious facility with latest equipment
- Affordable group training ($20-30/session)
- Flexible class schedules (early morning, lunch, evening)
- Welcoming, non-intimidating environment
- Technology integration (app-based tracking, virtual classes)
- Nutrition counseling and wellness programs
