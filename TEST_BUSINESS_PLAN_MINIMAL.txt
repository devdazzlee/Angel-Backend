BUSINESS PLAN - MINIMAL VERSION (FOR TESTING MISSING QUESTIONS)
================================================================

COMPANY INFORMATION
-------------------
Business Name: GreenLeaf Organic Farm
Industry: Agriculture & Organic Food Production
Location: Austin, Texas

MISSION
-------
To provide fresh, organic, locally-grown produce to our community while promoting sustainable farming practices.

PROBLEM
-------
Many consumers want fresh, organic produce but have limited access to locally-sourced options. Most organic food in stores is expensive and shipped from far away, losing nutritional value.

SOLUTION
--------
GreenLeaf Organic Farm will grow and sell organic vegetables, fruits, and herbs directly to consumers through farmers markets, CSA (Community Supported Agriculture) programs, and local restaurants.

TARGET MARKET
-------------
- Health-conscious families in Austin area
- Local restaurants seeking fresh ingredients
- Individuals interested in supporting local agriculture
- CSA program subscribers

COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE
---------------------
- 100% organic certification
- Direct-to-consumer model (no middlemen)
- Community-focused approach
- Seasonal variety of produce

PRICING
-------
- Farmers market: $3-$8 per item
- CSA subscription: $30/week for 20 weeks
- Restaurant wholesale: 20% discount from retail

MARKETING
---------
- Weekly farmers markets
- Social media presence
- Local restaurant partnerships
- Community events and workshops

OPERATIONS
----------
Location: 10-acre farm in Austin, Texas
Equipment: Tractor, irrigation system, greenhouse, storage facilities

STAFFING
--------
- 2 full-time farm workers
- 1 part-time farmers market salesperson
- Seasonal interns during harvest

FINANCIAL
---------
Startup Costs: $75,000
- Land lease: $15,000/year
- Equipment: $40,000
- Seeds and supplies: $10,000
- Working capital: $10,000

Monthly Expenses: $8,000
- Salaries: $5,000
- Supplies: $2,000
- Marketing: $1,000
